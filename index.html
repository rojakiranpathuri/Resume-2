ABOUT ME

Robin Hood is a legendary heroic outlaw originally depicted in English folklore and
subsequently featured in literature, theatre and cinema. According to legend, he was a
highly skilled archer and swordsman. In some versions of the legend, he is depicted as
being noble birth, and in modern retellings he is sometimes depicted as having fought in
the Crusades before returning to England to find his lands taken by the Sheriff. In the
oldest known versions, he is instead a member of the yeoman class. Traditionally depicted
dressed in Lincoin green, he is said to have stolen from the rich to give to the poor.
